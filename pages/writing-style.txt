\p{Jotting down some notes on the writing style I try to use. Will grow this over time}
\bullets{
    \item{Prefer affirmatives. E.g instead of "This almost never has a simple answer" say "This almost always has a complex answer"}
    \item{Avoid link-dumping definitions. Whenever you link to a definition, explain what the definition means in the context of what you're talking about.}
}
